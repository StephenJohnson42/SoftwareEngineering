Lore

How do I beat the game?

To win the game you have to get to the end of the labyrinth without dying. To progress down the floors you will have to collect a thousand gold per floor to get to the next one. The way to win is to get to the fifth floor.

Why are so many aspects of the game random?

They are random because the game is a roguelike video game. That is a genre that is based on having a randomly genereated map and randomly generated enemies so that the play experience will be different every time you play the game.

Will we be making any more games in the near future?

Considering that this was a project for Software Engineering class, I doubt there will be a Beneath the Manor 2, but considering how well our group worked together, there's always a possibility!

What did we use to make the website look so great?

We used a combination of Bootstrap style sheets, some custom style sheets, as well as a little bit of JavaScript to tie everything together.

What language was the game programmed in?

It was programmed in Java.
